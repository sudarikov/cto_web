Build the Culture™

Today, one of the biggest challenges for organizational leaders is to attract, acquire, develop, retain, and promote the right talent who possess the critical traits to build the desired corporate culture. Employees are loyal to culture not strategy, and this is true for recruitment as well. Companies that have an organizational culture that is both strong and healthy are more successful in recruiting, retaining, and growing talent.

When you decided to build a team in offshore location it brings even more complexity like in this story. We have a lot of experience setting up remote teams for our partners from all around the world and we are happy to help you build a great offshore team that will be natural extension to your core team.

Build the Culture™ is the package of services aimed to let you focus on your core business while we take care of everything else including:

  • Understand your core values and align them with business strategy
  • Create employer value-added proposition and your brand as an employer
  • Job descriptions to attract the right talent
  • Hiring a great team
  • On-boarding and get the team up to speed
  • Involve external consultants to build missing expertise in your team
  • Establish smooth communication between onshore and offshore teams
  • Define success metrics and make sure team is performing well
  • Make sure everyone has great personal and professional development plan
  • Develop rewards program aligned with values
  • Build strong local leadership via mentoring and coaching
  • Take care of office infrastructure, legal aspects, travels and payrolls*
