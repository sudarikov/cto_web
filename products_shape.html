Shape the Culture™

Every company needs to grow and scale. Before you go to the next level of your company evolution you want to make sure you build on top of solid basement.

This product will help you understand your "as-is" culture, develop even further your cultural strengths and compose it to Culture Framework™ which will get your organization to the next level smoothly.

The Culture Framework™ will enable everyone within your teams to make better decisions every day simply because they will be aligned not just with context, but with your core values as well.

What we will do:

  • Understand behaviors, beliefs and underlying assumptions
  • Setup culture ownership group
  • Define values and principles
  • Communicate values and principles across the estate
  • Align rewards strategy with values
  • Enhance hiring strategy based on values
  • Adjust on-boarding process
