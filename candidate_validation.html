Candidate validation

Recruitment is one of the most crucial processes for any organisation. Get the people wrong, and not only is there enormous financial damage, but reputation damage and loss of opportunity. The depth of knowledge required in the vast array of technology as well as the ability to dynamically be able to dig deeper to know the talent’s true capabilities requires unique insights.

We have helped numerous Corporates and Government departments validate the technical capabilitity of interviewees for positions ranging from Technology Directors to Technical Architects; from Team Leaders to Lead Developers. We are often called in when the initial interviews have been done – the shortlist decided and now the capabilities must be validated.

If you would like to ensure that the talent you are recruiting is technically capable and can deliver on the promise, contact us and we would be delighted to help you with the interview process.
