CTO as a Service

The Chief Technology Officer is the most important technology role your organisation will have. The role is also a conduit for communications between the business and technology functions of each company.

Your CTO should be a person that understands business strategy and the context of your business and have the right technical grasp of people, processes, systems and nuances of the latest technologies.

Here is some areas we can help you with:

Desing, Build & Debug Engineering Culture

  • Vision, values and goal-setting
  • Hiring, on-boarding, retention, development and motivation
  • Inspire teamwork, collaboration and innovation
  • Stakeholders and upward management
  • Improve feedback circles, delegation and decision-making process

Architect & Maintain Quality Technology Products

  • Software architecture and engineering practices
  • Platforms and technologies
  • Security and data privacy
  • Research and development
  • Data science
  • Quality assurance

Create Sustainable Operations, Process & Infrastructure

  • Product management and roadmapping
  • Process and release planning, Agile and Lean
  • Infrastructure, performance and ops
  • Continuous delivery and release management
  • Disaster recovery and business continuity planning
  • Budgets and resource planning
  • Security, governance and risk
