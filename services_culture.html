Culture as Usual™

This service will be the best for the companies which have remote teams and don't want to setup HR department in remote locations.

Our culture consultants will execute full-cycle HR activities in remote location under steering from your onshore HR team:

  • Hiring: coordinate activities with recruitment agencies
  • On-boarding: make every new member productive from day one
  • Performance management: annual or semi-annual performance reviews
  • Talent management: ensure every person stays happy, productive and motivated
  • Compensation and benefits: develop custom rewards strategy based on your company values
  • Training and development: build personal development plans, access to trainings and education
  • Transparent reporting: we will provide regular and transparent reports as for how your team is doing
