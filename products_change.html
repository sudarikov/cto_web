Change the Culture™

This product is designed to change your existing culture due to extensive company growth, M&A or just changed business strategy or market position.

What we will do:

  • Complex ‘as-is’ culture assessment & diagnostic
  • Fully understand the reason and inevitability of change
  • Form new culture vision and align it with business strategy, current & future market position
  • Identify strong existing values which will support change
  • Evaluate risks and prepare mitigation plan
  • Define scope of change and milestones.
  • Build communication plan
  • Announce change, align processes, align organisational structure, align rewards strategy
  • Work with leaders on every level to make change sustainable
